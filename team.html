Capstone

COAL was originally developed as a 2016 – 2017 Oregon State University (OSU) senior capstone project. COAL development continued as a 2017 – 2018 senior capstone collaboration and community Free and Open Source Software project. The senior capstone is a three-term software engineering project led by industry professionals and developed by undergraduate computer science students at OSU. Visit the course homepage for more details or keep reading to learn about our team.

Contact

The best way to reach us is to contact us directly by email or to visit our GitHub Organization. Our geographically distributed team meets regularly by videoconference and collaborates on research and development primarily through our mailing list. You may view our messages on Google Groups or ask to join, however posting is by invitation only.
