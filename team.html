Senior Capstone

COAL is being developed as a 2016 – 2017 senior capstone collaboration between the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) and Oregon State University (OSU). The senior capstone is a three-term design project led by industry professionals and developed by undergraduate computer science students at OSU. Visit the course homepage for more details or keep reading to learn about our team.

Contact

The best way to reach us is to contact us directly by email or to visit our GitHub Organization. Our geographically distributed team meets regularly by videoconference and collaborates on research and development primarily through our mailing list. You may view our messages on Google Groups or ask to join, however only invited people will be able to post.
